# SlideRev: The Essential Pro Extension for AI Slide Users

**From Flattened Citations to Fully Editable Presentations.**

Are you using **NotebookLM** or other AI tools to generate insightful slides and infographics? While AI is powerful, its outputs are often "read-only" images or PDFs, sometimes containing typos or unwanted watermarks that prevent them from being "client-ready."

**SlideRev** is the native macOS solution designed to bridge this gap. It's the must-have companion for anyone looking to turn AI-generated snapshots back into professional, high-fidelity PowerPoint (PPTX) and PDF documents.

## 🔥 Key System Features

### 1. AI-Powered Vision OCR

Don't just extract text—reconstruct it. SlideRev identifies not only the characters but also their **exact position, font size, and color**. It performs a "one-to-one" replacement, turning blurry pixels into crisp, editable text layers.

### 2. High-Fidelity LaMa Inpainting

Say goodbye to "Sample Only" watermarks or AI-generated typos. Using the industry-leading **LaMa** neural model, SlideRev intelligently erases unwanted Foreground elements and reconstructs the background texture with seamless precision.

### 3. Native OOXML PPTX Engine

No Microsoft Office required. SlideRev generates industrial-standard `.pptx` files from scratch using a native XML injection engine, ensuring 100% compatibility with PowerPoint and Keynote.

### 4. 100% Local & Private

Your documents never leave your Mac. All AI inference and PDF processing happen entirely offline, ensuring maximum privacy for your sensitive research and business data.

## 📖 User Guide: 3 Steps to Perfection

SlideRev is designed for speed. Transform your AI citations in seconds:

1. **Import**: Drag and drop your NotebookLM PDF, screenshot, or infographic into the SlideRev dashboard.
2. **Refine**: Click the **"Refine"** button. SlideRev will automatically identify text blocks and background watermarks. You can even use the **Eraser Tool** for manual pixel-perfect touch-ups.
3. **Export**: Choose between **"Editable PPTX"** for deep modification in PowerPoint or **"High-Quality PDF"** for professional sharing.

## 🛡️ Privacy Policy

SlideRev is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy.

- **Offline Processing**: We do not use any cloud-based API for OCR or Inpainting. Everything is powered by CoreML on your local CPU/GPU.
- **No Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any part of your uploaded documents or the text extracted from them.
- **Sandboxed Security**: SlideRev fully complies with macOS App Sandbox requirements, ensuring it only accesses the files you explicitly provide.
